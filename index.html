Dodgy Deals
The Collins Street Sellout

Melbourne's founder* John Batman,
takes you on a walking tour of Melbourne's richest street.

Find Out More

*It's copmpletely disengenuous to state that John Batman founded Melbourne, considering the evidence of indigenous settlement in the area going back as far as 40,000 years.

John Batman is back from the dead, and he wants to explain.

So why not have him better to give you the story on Collins Street, Melbourne's wealthiest boulevard? After all, this is where the deals are done that decide the future of Melbourne and its 5 million residents.

Batman will talk about the deals that made and broke Melbourne, even when Melbourne was broke already. Who get's free rent at cost of the public? Despite Melbourne claiming to be a green left paradise, there are legal exemptions on access rights to some minorities ? What happened to the City Square?

Join this controversial figure on a evening stroll through Melbourne as part of the 2019 Melbourne Fringe Festival.
